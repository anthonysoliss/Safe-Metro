Your Privacy Matters
Privacy Policy
This policy explains what information Ratemetro collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. We believe in full transparency — no hidden practices, no selling your data.
Information We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information — username, email address, and optional profile photo when you register
  • Station ratings — your safety and cleanliness scores, descriptions, and any photos you attach to ratings
  • Location data — approximate GPS coordinates when you use the locate feature or share your location (only if you grant permission)
  • Usage activity — pages visited and actions taken, used solely to improve the app
  • Device information — browser type and IP address, used for security and analytics
How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Ratemetro:

  • Display station ratings and reviews on the map
  • Personalise your experience (home station, preferences)
  • Show you nearby stations based on your location
  • Send you replies to feedback or support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Analyse anonymous usage trends to improve the app
Ratings & Anonymity

By default, your username is shown alongside ratings you submit. You can switch to anonymous mode at any time from Settings → Privacy → Anonymous Ratings. When enabled, your name is hidden from all public rating displays.

Anonymous ratings are still linked to your account internally for moderation purposes, but are never publicly attributed to you.

Location Data

Location access is entirely optional. We request it only when you tap the locate button or enable location sharing. Your coordinates are used to:

  • Sort stations by distance from you
  • Attach location metadata to ratings you submit
  • Improve station coverage based on anonymised heatmaps

You can disable location sharing at any time from Settings → Privacy → Share Location or via your browser/device settings. We do not track your location in the background.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help operate infrastructure (hosting, email delivery) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data would transfer under the same privacy terms
Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal information — including your ratings, avatar, and profile — is permanently removed within 30 days. Aggregated, anonymised analytics data may be retained longer.

Location logs older than 90 days are automatically purged.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access — view all data we hold about you from your profile and ratings pages
  • Export — request a copy of your data via Settings → Privacy → Export My Data
  • Correction — update your username, email, or avatar at any time from your profile
  • Deletion — permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Danger Zone
  • Opt-out — disable analytics tracking from Settings → Privacy at any time
Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data: HTTPS encryption in transit, hashed passwords, and access controls limiting who can view personal information. No system is 100% secure — if you suspect a breach, please contact us immediately.

Contact & Questions

If you have questions about this policy or want to exercise any of your rights, reach out using the feedback form or the email below.

Have a privacy concern? Send us a message and we'll respond within 48 hours.
