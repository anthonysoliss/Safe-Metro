Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ratemetro.com.

ratemetro.com is a community-powered platform for Los Angeles Metro riders. It provides AI-powered safety ratings, real-time train arrivals, and walking directions for every station on the LA Metro system.

Safety ratings are based on reviews submitted by real riders in the community. Each station's rating reflects the average of all user-submitted scores, giving you an honest picture of what to expect.

Yes, ratemetro.com is completely free. You can browse the map, view safety ratings, and check real-time arrivals without an account. Creating an account lets you submit your own reviews and save your preferences.

ratemetro.com covers all seven LA Metro rail lines: A (Blue), B (Red), C (Green), D (Purple), E (Expo), G (Orange), and K (Crenshaw) — including every station on each line.

We pull live departure data directly from the LA Metro API, so you see up-to-the-minute arrival times for every station. Just tap on any station on the map to view upcoming trains.
